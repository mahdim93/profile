I'm a Ph.D. researcher in the Department of Industrial Systems Engineering and Product Design at Ghent University, supervised by Prof. Sidharta Gautama. My work sits at the intersection of operations research, probabilistic reasoning, and smart manufacturing — building decision-support systems that make quality management in Industry 4.0 more intelligent and human-centered.
